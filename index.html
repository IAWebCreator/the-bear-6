HOW TO GET YOUR PAWS ON $TB

1

CREATE A WALLET

Download MetaMask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

2

GET SOME ETH

Have ETH in your wallet to swap for $TB. If you don’t have any ETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

3

GO TO UNISWAP

Connect to Uniswap. Go to app.uniswap.org in google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $TB token address into Uniswap, select TB, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

4

SWAP ETH FOR $TB

Swap ETH for $TB. We have ZERO taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
